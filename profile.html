Profile

International Trade House is a New Delhi based full-service buying office. Since our inception in 1994, we have built a strong reputation for delivering high quality products on time. We represent foreign chain stores, discount stores and importers who source a range of garments and other products from India.

Product Range

We supply a wide range of outerwear and casual wear products for men, women and children. The product range includes the following e.g. Knit tops, casual tops, golf apparel, t shirts/sweats, casual Pant, casual shirts, shorts, cargo pants, loungewear, sweaters.

Customers Profile

We represent importers to chain stores in Europe, USA and Canada and have been successfully supplying to them for the last twenty three years.

Business Purview

We manage all aspects of clients’ buying operations in India. These include: Sourcing, Sampling, Negotiation, Communication, Approvals, Inspections, Documentaion

Not only does our team of merchandisers and QA personnel ensure quality products and timely deliveries; our quality controllers inspect the vendors regularly to carry out production checks so that quality is not compromised. This keeps us abreast of all the developments, which enable us to keep our customer informed about their production on a regular basis. This also empowers us to have the much needed control to ensure production matches the customers’ specifications and the delivery schedules are met. ITH sources products from vendors after a careful assessment of their factories. These assessments are based on the human rights certification,equipment and machinery installations, prevalent working procedures, level of professionalism and includes creation of a rating grade to the factory, which can determine the quantity of garments it can manufacture for ITH as well as whether it has adequate production capacity.
